Certification

Advanced assessment and progress monitoring tool aligned with international standards. It provides continuous evaluations, and certification simulations, ensuring effective learning and measurable outcomes such as:
  • Targeted assessments: Listening and Reading.
  • Detailed feedback: Identifies strengths and areas for improvement.
  • Customized reports: Advanced analytics for institutions and educators.
  • Simulations: Practical preparation for exams like TOEIC®.

Professional
Development

ED Community is a virtual hub for English language teachers, where they can learn, share, and grow together.

Our online community offers micro-courses on key topics such as student motivation, educational technology, and new approaches to teaching grammar and vocabulary.

Teachers can explore methods, strategies, and tools for virtual learning. At the end of each course, they complete a questionnaire to assess their understanding and, upon passing, receive a micro-certification.
